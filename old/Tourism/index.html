Wide Mountains

Dec 8, 2020

After one days of travelling from Delhi,India. We finally reached our destination Darjeeling , West bengal.... It is located in the Lesser Himalayas at an elevation of 2,000 metres (6,700 ft). The best part of the trip to Darjeeling was its tea industry, its views of Kangchenjunga, the world's third-highest mountain, and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. In Darjeeling we also visited the headquarters of the Darjeeling district which has a partially autonomous status called Gorkhaland Territorial Administration within the state of West Bengal. It is also a popular tourist destination in India.
